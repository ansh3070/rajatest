Marigold Flowers

Marigolds are vibrant, easy-to-grow annual flowers that bring brilliant colors of orange, red, and yellow to any garden. These hardy blooms are perfect for beginners and are known for their pest-repelling properties and long blooming season from spring to frost.

How to Care for Marigold Flowers

Growth Information

  • Best Season to Grow: Spring to Early Summer
  • Growth Rate: Fast growing, blooms in 6-8 weeks from seed
  • Sunlight Requirement: Full sun (6+ hours daily)

Plant Care Instructions

  • Watering: Water regularly but allow soil to dry between waterings.
  • Fertilizer: Light feeding with balanced fertilizer monthly.
  • Soil: Well-draining soil, tolerates poor soil conditions.
  • Deadheading: Remove spent blooms to encourage continuous flowering.
